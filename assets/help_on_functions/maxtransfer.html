MaxTransfer function

Actuarial Excel Addin

The MaxTransfer function, returns the factor to calculate the maximum amount that may be transferred to an RRSP, RRIF, or MP provision from a DB provision of an RPP as per paragraph 8517 of the Canada Income Tax Regulation.

* : Required parameter

Parameters
Default
Description
Age *
--
Age at the date of the transfer, fraction allowed

Example #1
The member has 45 years of age at the date of transfer.

Age = 45

MaxTransfer(Age) returns 9.0


Example #2
The member has 60.8 years of age at the date of transfer.

Age = 60.8

MaxTransfer(Age) returns 11.66
