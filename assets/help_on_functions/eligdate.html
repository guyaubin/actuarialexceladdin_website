EligDate function

Actuarial Excel Addin

The EligDate function, returns the first date where a set of conditions are met (e.g. minimum age, minimum service or minimum age + service).

EligDate is used when you have all the dates, e.g. date of birth, date of hire, date of membership.

See also EligDate2, when you have the age and service at a given date.

* : Required parameter

Parameters
Default
Description
Date 1 *
--
e.g. date of birth
Min 1 *
--
First criteria: Minimum duration elapsed after Date 1 (in years), fraction allowed
Date 2
--
e.g. date of hire or date joined plan
Min 2
--
Second criteria: Minimum duration elapsed after Date 2 (in years), fraction allowed
Min 1-2
--
Third criteria: Minimum sum of duration elapsed since Date 1 and Date 2 (in years), fraction allowed
MeetAll
False
True: Must meet all three criterias - False: Must meet any of the criteria
NextMonth
True
If date is not 1st of the month, return next 1st of the month

Example #1
Normal retirement at age 60 (next 1st of the month) for a person born on July 15, 1979:

Date1 = date(1979, 7, 15)

Eligdate(Date1, 60) returns 2039-08-01


Example #2

Unreduced early retirement when the following conditions are met:

  1. Minimum 55 years of age
  2. Minimum 30 years of service
  3. Minimum age + service is 90 years

Date1 = date(1979, 7, 15)

Date2 = date(2006, 11, 28)

Eligdate(Date1, 55, Date2, 30, 90, True, True) returns 2038-04-01
