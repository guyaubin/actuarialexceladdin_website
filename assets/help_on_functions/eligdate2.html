EligDate2 function

Actuarial Excel Addin

The EligDate2 function, returns the first date where a set of conditions are met (e.g. minimum age, minimum service or minimum age + service).

EligDate2 is used when you have the accrued period, e.g. age, service at a given date.

See also EligDate, when you have all the dates.

* : Required parameter

Parameters
Default
Description
Date *
--
Date at which accrued periods are calculated
Dur 1 *
--
Duration 1 in years (e.g. age), fraction allowed
Min 1 *
--
First criteria: Minimum duration 1 (in years), fraction allowed
Dur 2
--
Duration 2 in years (e.g. service, membership), fraction allowed
Min 2
--
Second criteria: Minimum duration 2 (in years), fraction allowed
Min 1-2
--
Third criteria: Minimum sum of duration 1 and 2 (in years), fraction allowed
MeetAll
False
True: Must meet all three criterias - False: Must meet any of the criteria
NextMonth
True
If date is not 1st of the month, return next 1st of the month

Example #1: Normal retirement at age 60 (next 1st of the month)

On December 31, 2020, the member has 41.46 years of age.

DateNow = date(2020, 12, 31)

Eligdate2(DateNow, 41.46, 60) = 2039-08-01


Example #2: Unreduced early retirement when the following conditions are met:

  1. Minimum 55 years of age
  2. Minimum 30 years of service
  3. Minimum age + service is 90 years

On December 31, 2020, the member has 41.46 years of age and 14.09 years of service.

DateNow = date(2020, 12, 31)

Eligdate2(DateNow, 41.46, 55, 14.09, 30, 90, True, True) = 2038-04-01
