CERTAIN function

Actuarial Excel Addin

The CERTAIN function returns the value at time = 0 of an annuity certain paying $1 per year during Duration year(s) starting at Deferred, discounted with interest and payable:

  • with or without indexation, and
  • with or without reduction after a contigent annuitant's death.

* : Required parameter
