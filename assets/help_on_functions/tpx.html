TPX function

Actuarial Excel Addin

The TPX function, from the \(_{t}p_x\) actuarial notation (where \(_{t}p_x={l_{x+t}\over l_x}\)), returns the probability of survival from Age (\(x\)) to Age + NbYears (\(x+t\)).

See also: TQX, LifeExpectancy

* : Required parameter
