TaxStat function

Actuarial Excel Addin

See the tutorial on Youtube

The TaxStat function returns, for years since 1990, the Canadian Income Tax Act limits for Registered Pension Plans (RPP), including Defined benefit (DB), Defined Contribution (DC), Registered Retirement Savings Plan (RRSP) and Deferred Profit Sharing Plan (DPSP).
